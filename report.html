Goals

The main goal is to raise awareness of the Barrier Reef Orchestra and  increase the number of people attending the concerts and getting involved in the Orchestra itself. The site should also help to increase the number of sponsors for the orchestra, and conductors showing interest in getting involved.

PMI

Pluses: The work load was slip which allowed each team member to focus on certain aspects of the website to achieve a higher quality of work. All team members were able to share different ideas on website design and layout and were respectful of each other's opinions. We were able to learn from each other and improve our individual knowledge of web design by working in this group environment.

Minuses: Trying to designate times for group meetings that were suitable for every member and adjusted to their other commitments was often difficult. Apart from this there were no major issues to speak of, as the team worked really well together.

Interesting: It was interesting to see how well we worked together as a group despite none of us knowing each other before hand. Each team member had enough empathy to complete the assignment without any disputes between group members. Problems were resolved quickly with no arguments. It was also interesting to observe how individual team members used different methods to complete given tasks, and we were able to learn from each other as a result.
